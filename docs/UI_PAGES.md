# UI Pages (Dashboard)

The React-based dashboard allows business owners to manage their operations efficiently.

## 1. Authentication
- **Login / Signup**: Handled via Supabase Auth.
- **Onboarding**: Multi-step form to set up business profile and services.

## 2. Dashboard Home
- **Stats Overview**: Total bookings today, monthly revenue, average rating.
- **Quick Actions**: Add manual booking, generate QR code.

## 3. Appointments Page
- **Calendar View**: Drag-and-drop interface for rescheduling (future).
- **List View**: Sortable and filterable table of all upcoming and past bookings.

## 4. Services Page
- **CRUD Operations**: Manage services offered, durations, and pricing.
- **Availability**: Set business hours and holidays.

## 5. Reviews & Feedback
- **Inbox**: View all customer ratings and private comments.
- **Analytics**: Trend chart of customer satisfaction.

## 6. Settings & Billing
- **Business Profile**: QR code downloader and shop info.
- **Subscription**: Manage Razorpay plans and invoice history.
